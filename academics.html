Our Academic Programs

At Golden Tots Academy, we follow the Competence-Based Curriculum (CBC) to ensure holistic development for our students. Our programs include:

  • Playgroup: Early childhood education for toddlers.
  • PP1 and PP2: Pre-primary education to prepare children for primary school.
  • Grade 1 to Grade 6: Primary education focusing on core subjects and life skills.
  • Junior Secondary (Grade 7-9): Advanced learning to prepare students for high school.

Extracurricular Activities

We offer a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports, music, drama, and clubs, to help students explore their talents and interests.

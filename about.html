About Golden Tots Academy

Located in Likuyani Subcounty, Kenya, Golden Tots Academy is a leading institution dedicated to providing quality education through the Competence-Based Curriculum (CBC). Our programs cater to learners from Playgroup to Junior Secondary (Grade 7-9), nurturing their talents and preparing them for a bright future.
